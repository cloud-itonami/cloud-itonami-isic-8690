Other human health activities

ISIC Rev.5 8690

The Problem: Allied-health practices face a false choice: adopt a SaaS platform and lose patient data sovereignty, or build compliance infrastructure from scratch.

The Solution: An open-source, forkable business blueprint that bundles the compliance layer—encounter intake, assessment verification, practitioner screening, and treatment administration—so any qualified practice can deploy it on their own infrastructure.

The Differentiator: An independent Allied Health Governor (not an LLM) enforces four hard safety gates: spec-basis citation, complete evidence, scope-of-practice membership, and current credentials. Treatment delivery requires mandatory human approval. No unsafe commits ever ship.

What it is NOT: This is not a clinical decision-making system, a practice-management replacement, or a substitute for licensed practitioners. Whoever operates it in a jurisdiction supplies the license, workforce, and liability.

Any qualified, licensed allied-health practice (physiotherapy, chiropractic, optometry, ambulance, or paramedical services) can fork and deploy this under AGPL-3.0 — keeping patient data on your own terms while maintaining regulatory compliance.
