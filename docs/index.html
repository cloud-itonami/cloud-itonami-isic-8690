Other human health activities

ISIC Rev.5 8690

An open business blueprint for allied-health operators: encounter intake, assessment verification, practitioner-credential screening, and treatment-session administration.

Deployed as an LLM-driven actor wrapped with an independent Allied Health Governor, immutable audit ledger, and mandatory human sign-off for treatment delivery — no patient data leaves your infrastructure.

Any qualified, licensed allied-health practice (physiotherapy, chiropractic, optometry, ambulance, or paramedical services) can fork, deploy, self-host, and operate this blueprint under AGPL-3.0 — eliminating the choice between regulatory safety and data sovereignty.
